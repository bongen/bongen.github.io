Biography

While steadily standing on the ground of traditional jazz, he is in a constant exploration of rhythm and sound, with improvised music being at its core. Desire to dive deeper led him from Siberia to Europe and New York City.

Evgeny Bondarev is a Russian jazz trumpeter, born in 1985 in West Siberia, currently based in Brazil. Before moving to the land of samba and the berimbau, he spent five years in The Netherlands, studying and absorbing the vibe of the European jazz scene.

In 2014, he received his master's degree in Jazz Performance from Conservatorium van Amsterdam. Shortly after, Evgeny enrolled in the New York Jazz master program at Prince Claus Conservatoire in Groningen. While following the program, he spent six months in New York City, taking private lessons.
